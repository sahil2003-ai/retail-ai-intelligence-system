# 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Insights

## 📌 Data Patterns
- Sales are consistent across months with slight seasonal variation
- Peak demand observed in July and October
- February shows a noticeable dip

---

## 📈 Distributions
- Revenue is evenly distributed across categories
- Inventory levels show imbalance (overstock vs low stock)
- Pricing is tightly clustered (~₹55 range)

---

## 🔗 Correlations
- Positive correlation between price and revenue (moderate)
- Strong relationship between demand forecast and units sold
- Inventory level weakly correlated with sales → inefficiency

---

## 📦 Category Analysis
- Top categories: Furniture, Groceries
- Electronics maintain stable pricing advantage
- Clothing shows underpricing trend

---

## 🌍 Regional Patterns
- Revenue evenly distributed across regions
- No strong regional dominance → diversified market

---

## 💡 Business Meaning
- Stable demand → mature market
- Inventory mismatch → operational inefficiency
- Pricing strategy → safe but not optimized
- Opportunity for demand-driven inventory planning
