# 📊 Dashboard Explanation – Retail AI Intelligence System

## 🧠 Overview

The dashboard is designed to provide a **comprehensive view of retail business performance** across key domains:

- Sales
- Inventory
- Pricing
- Demand Forecasting

It enables **data-driven decision-making** through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To transform raw retail data into actionable insights by:

- Monitoring key business metrics
- Identifying inefficiencies
- Supporting strategic decisions

---

# 📌 Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is divided into **5 main sections**:

---

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

### 📊 Purpose:
Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

### 🔹 Key KPIs:
- Total Revenue
- Total Units Sold
- Average Price
- Estimated Profit
- Low Stock Count

### 📈 Visuals:
- Revenue Trend (Monthly)
- Revenue by Category (Donut Chart)
- Revenue by Region (Map)
- Top Products by Revenue (Bar Chart)

### 💡 Insight:
Gives a quick snapshot of overall business health.

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Analytics

### 📊 Purpose:
Analyze revenue performance and sales trends.

### 🔹 Key Metrics:
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Orders
- Sales Growth %

### 📈 Visuals:
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Revenue by Category (Bar Chart)
- Region-wise Sales (Donut Chart)
- Product Performance (Pie Chart)

### 💡 Insight:
- Identifies best-selling products
- Detects seasonal patterns
- Highlights growth opportunities

---

## 3️⃣ Inventory Analytics

### 📊 Purpose:
Monitor stock levels and inventory efficiency.

### 🔹 Key Metrics:
- Total Inventory
- Stock Turnover
- Low Stock Count
- Overstock Count

### 📈 Visuals:
- Inventory vs Sales (Combo Chart)
- Inventory Risk Distribution (Donut Chart)
- Stock Turnover by Category (Bar Chart)
- Overstocked Products (Bar Chart)

### 💡 Insight:
- Detects inventory imbalance
- Highlights slow-moving products
- Helps optimize stock levels

---

## 4️⃣ Pricing Analytics

### 📊 Purpose:
Evaluate pricing strategies and market competitiveness.

### 🔹 Key Metrics:
- Average Price
- Average Discount %
- Price Difference vs Competitors
- Profit Margin %

### 📈 Visuals:
- Price vs Demand (Scatter Plot)
- Competitor Price Comparison (Bar Chart)
- Discount Impact on Sales (Bar Chart)
- Price Difference Analysis (Bar Chart)

### 💡 Insight:
- Identifies underpricing or overpricing
- Evaluates impact of discounts on sales
- Supports profit optimization

---

## 5️⃣ Demand Forecasting

### 📊 Purpose:
Predict future demand and analyze forecasting accuracy.

### 🔹 Key Metrics:
- Total Forecast
- Forecast Accuracy %
- Demand Gap
- Error %

### 📈 Visuals:
- Forecast vs Actual (Line Chart)
- Monthly Forecast View (Bar Chart)
- Seasonal Trend (Line Chart)
- Error Distribution (Donut Chart)

### 💡 Insight:
- Evaluates model performance
- Identifies demand patterns
- Supports inventory planning

---
